About This Event

Join us for an unforgettable evening at the Royal Garden Wedding Gala, where elegance meets celebration in the heart of New York City. Set within the stunning Grand Palazzo, this event promises a night of refined dining, enchanting entertainment, and timeless memories.

From the moment you step through the grand entrance adorned with cascading florals and soft candlelight, you'll be transported to a world of sophistication. Our world-class culinary team has curated a five-course menu featuring seasonal delicacies paired with premium wines from renowned vineyards.

The evening features a live orchestra performance, a mesmerizing dance showcase, and a breathtaking fireworks finale under the stars. Whether you're celebrating love, making new connections, or simply enjoying an extraordinary night out, the Royal Garden Wedding Gala is an experience you won't want to miss.
