❓ FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an event on EventVerse? +
Creating an event is easy! Click the "Create Event" button in the navigation bar, fill in your event details across our 4-step wizard (Details → Venue → Tickets → Review), and hit publish. Your event will be live on EventVerse within minutes.
Is EventVerse free to use? +
EventVerse is free for attendees to browse and book events. Event organizers can create events for free, with a small service fee applied to paid ticket sales. We also offer premium plans with advanced analytics, priority support, and custom branding options.
How does vendor verification work? +
All vendors on EventVerse go through a thorough verification process. We check their business credentials, review their portfolio, and collect feedback from past clients. Verified vendors receive a ✅ badge on their profile, giving organizers confidence in their quality and reliability.
What payment methods are accepted? +
We accept all major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and bank transfers. All payments are processed securely with industry-standard encryption and PCI-DSS compliance.
Can I cancel or reschedule my event? +
Yes! Event organizers can cancel or reschedule their events from the event management dashboard. Attendees will be automatically notified of any changes. Refund policies vary — organizers can set their own refund terms, which are displayed on each event page.
How do I contact support? +
You can reach our support team via email at support@eventverse.com, by phone at +1 (800) 555-EVENT during business hours, or by filling out the contact form above. Our average response time is under 4 hours during business days.
